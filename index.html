Welcome to COVINT!
Our app is a new way to find and experience local fun. Using this app, all sorts of new possibilities open up for fun and excitement near you. COVINT is your gateway to immersive local experiences!
COVINT allows a person to create a profile based on their interests, which in turn allows us to help you find your fun! COVINT offers an exciting way to connect and explore. We encourage our users to explore what their city has to offer and to turn downtime into something memorable!
Our profile system is that first step that will allow you to both enjoy things you already like, or find new hobbies for yourself. You get to experience personalized moments that matter, with the people that matter most to you.
Discover events or groups, meet new friends or connect with old ones, and explore your interests, all in one place! Our app will give you the ability to connect over meaningful activities, bringing family and friends closer together! The easiest way to find your people is just a few steps away! Just head over to Discover Interests to get started, or go to Find Your Fun to learn more.
